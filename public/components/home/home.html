About Our Application


The Idea
We started by coming up with an idea to digitalize and personalize a varsatile to-do list. By forgetting about the pen-and-paper style of noting things down (and not missing it), GoalMind is an easy and minimalistic app that can help anyone stay on track with life's many tasks.

With this application, our users can simply create goals and complete them either at home at a desktop, at the coffee shop on a laptop, or on-the-go on their phone. Take GoalMind with you and aim for the top!


  • Learn More on How to Get Started

How GoalMind Works


Get going easily and quickly with our simple set-up to start creating your goals, earning points, and feeling accomplished.



Step 1.

Begin by heading over to the side navigation and look for the "New Goal" menu item. Click it, and start filling the form with all the details you want to specify your goal. For example, you can make your goal repeat a few times a week, or set a duedate.



Step 2.

After setting up some goals for yourself, start getting them done! Once you feel you have completed a goal or a task, hit the "complete" button. Now you are on your way to clearing your schedule and kicking procrastination to the curb!



Step 3.

After each fully completed goal, you will earn points according to how quickly you have complete it. Rack up enough points, and you will level up! Challenge yourself to try to get to the highest level you can achieve.
